<h3 className="font-sans text-xs font-semibold uppercase tracking-[0.15em] text-accent mt-8 mb-3">Explanation</h3>
<p className="mb-6">There is a peculiar paradox in knowledge: the more you know about a subject, the harder it becomes to explain it to someone who doesn't.</p>
<p className="mb-6">The art of explanation is not about demonstrating how much you know. It is about meeting someone where they are.</p>

<h3 className="font-sans text-xs font-semibold uppercase tracking-[0.15em] text-accent mt-8 mb-3">Example</h3>
<p className="mb-6">Consider Richard Feynman, the Nobel Prize-winning physicist known as "The Great Explainer."</p>

<blockquote className="border-l-2 border-accent pl-5 my-8 italic text-text-secondary">
    <p className="m-0">"If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough."</p>
</blockquote>

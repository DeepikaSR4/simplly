<h3 className="font-sans text-xs font-semibold uppercase tracking-[0.15em] text-accent mt-8 mb-3">Methodology</h3>
<p className="mb-6">The Feynman technique involves teaching a concept to a child. By forcing yourself to explain things without jargon, you quickly identify the gaps in your own understanding.</p>
<p className="mb-6">Modern tools allow us to instantly scale this technique. You can provide your complex notes and ask AI to translate it into an ELI5 framework, instantly giving you a mirror of your own knowledge base.</p>
